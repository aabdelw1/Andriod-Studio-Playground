  None.          [single][if !emptyfile]
  One.           [if !emptyfile][property nonAlphanumeric]

  Not Empty.    [property thereIsString]

  Yes.          [if !noString && !emptyfile && !doNothing]

	0.					[single][property noInteger]

	<0.			    [error]

Slice?: # (-t)
  Yes.      [if !doNothing && !emptyfile && posInt]
  No.       [if noInteger]

  Yes.        [if !emptyfile && !doNothing]
  No.
